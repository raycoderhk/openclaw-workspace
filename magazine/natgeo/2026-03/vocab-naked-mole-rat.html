🐀 The Naked Mole Rat

Vocabulary & Quiz | 詞彙與測驗

📖 Read Original Article →

📝 Article Summary | 文章摘要

The naked mole rat, a nearly blind rodent, can live for decades without showing signs of aging. Scientists like Rochelle Buffenstein study these creatures to unlock secrets of longevity, cancer resistance, and disease prevention that could benefit human health.

裸鼴鼠是一種幾乎失明的囓齒動物，可以存活數十年而不顯示衰老跡象。像 Rochelle Buffenstein 這樣的科學家研究這些生物，以解開長壽、抗癌同預防疾病的秘密，這些可能有益於人類健康。

longevity n. /lɒnˈdʒev.ə.ti/
Long life; the length of time a person or animal lives
長壽，壽命
"Humanity's longevity obsession explodes, fostered by an industry expected to surpass $44 billion by 2030."
人類對長壽的痴迷爆發式增長，由一個預計到 2030 年將超過 440 億美元的產業所推動。
eusocial adj. /juːˈsəʊ.ʃəl/
Living in organized colonies with division of labor and reproductive specialization
真社會性的（有勞動分工同繁殖專門化的群居）
"They were the first known eusocial mammal, meaning they live in a cooperative structure with labor division."
它們是已知的第一種真社會性哺乳動物，意味著它們生活在具有勞動分工的合作結構中。
diminishment n. /dɪˈmɪn.ɪʃ.mənt/
The act of making or becoming less; reduction
減少，減弱
"His oldest naked mole rat had no notable diminishment of muscle mass, bone density, or metabolism."
他最老的裸鼴鼠沒有明顯的肌肉質量、骨密度或新陳代謝的減弱。
vascular adj. /ˈvæs.kjə.lər/
Relating to the vessels that carry blood and other fluids
血管的，脈管的
"His vascular function stayed strong even at nearly 40 years old."
即使接近 40 歲，他的血管功能仍然強勁。
neurodegeneration n. /ˌnjʊə.rəʊ.dɪˌdʒen.əˈreɪ.ʃən/
The progressive loss of structure or function of neurons
神經退化，神經元功能喪失
"Her creatures showed few to no signs of stroke, neurodegeneration, diminished heart function, or cancer."
她的動物幾乎沒有中風、神經退化、心功能減弱或癌症的跡象。
anesthetic n. /ˌæn.əsˈθet.ɪk/
A substance that causes insensitivity to pain
麻醉劑
"She places him in a small box with tubes pumping in a vaporized anesthetic."
她將他放入一個小盒子，管子泵入霧化的麻醉劑。
menopause n. /ˈmen.ə.pɔːz/
The time in a female's life when menstruation stops permanently
更年期，停經期
"Females never go through menopause—they continue to breed until they die."
雌性從不經歷更年期——它們持續繁殖直到死亡。
cognitive adj. /ˈkɒɡ.nə.tɪv/
Relating to mental processes such as thinking, understanding, and learning
認知的，認識的
"Researchers seek antidotes to all the pitfalls of aging—cancer, heart attack, stroke, cognitive decline."
研究人員尋求對抗所有衰老陷阱的解藥——癌症、心臟病發作、中風、認知能力下降。
colony n. /ˈkɒl.ə.ni/
A group of organisms of the same species living together
群落，群體
"Each governed by a queen, the colonies live in plastic mouse and rat cages connected by tubing."
每個群落由一隻蟻后統治，生活在通過管子連接的塑料小鼠同大鼠籠子中。
juvenile adj. /ˈdʒuː.və.naɪl/
Relating to young people; immature or youthful
青少年的，幼稚的
"Naked mole rats maintain juvenile—even fetal—biological features inside and out as the years tick by."
隨著歲月流逝，裸鼴鼠在內外都保持著青少年甚至胎兒時期的生物特徵。
