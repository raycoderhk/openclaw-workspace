The Economist - Advanced English (with Chinese Translation)

📰 Article:China hopes IVF can slow its baby bust
📂 Section:China
📅 Date:February 28, 2026
📊 Level:Advanced (C1-C2)
🔗 Original:The Economist
🗄️ Archive:Archive.ph

🌍 Article Context | 文章背景

This article examines China's declining birth rate, which has fallen to one child per woman, and the government's strategy to subsidize IVF treatments through public insurance to encourage more births.

中文：這篇文章檢視了中國下降的出生率（已降至每名婦女一個孩子），以及政府通過公共保險補貼 IVF 治療以鼓勵更多生育的策略。

fertility n /fəˈtɪl.ə.ti/ The ability to produce children or young
n. 生育能力，肥沃
"China's fertility rate has fallen to one child per woman"
譯：中國的生育率已降至每名婦女一個孩子
subsidize v /ˈsʌb.sɪ.daɪz/ To pay part of the cost of something
v. 補貼，資助
"The government will subsidize IVF treatments"
譯：政府將補貼 IVF 治療
insurance n /ɪnˈʃɔː.rəns/ An arrangement for financial protection against loss
n. 保險
"IVF will be covered by public insurance"
譯：IVF 將由公共保險覆蓋
demographic adj/n /ˌdem.əˈɡræf.ɪk/ Relating to population statistics
adj. 人口的，統計的
n. 人口統計
"Demographic challenges face China"
譯：中國面臨人口挑戰
decline n/v /dɪˈklaɪn/ A gradual decrease; to become smaller
n. 下降，衰退
v. 減少，拒絕
"The birth rate continues to decline"
譯：出生率持續下降
conceive v /kənˈsiːv/ To become pregnant
v. 懷孕，構想
"Many couples struggle to conceive naturally"
譯：許多夫婦難以自然懷孕
treatment n /ˈtriːt.mənt/ Medical care given to a patient
n. 治療，處理
"IVF treatment is expensive"
譯：IVF 治療費用昂貴
incentive n /ɪnˈsen.tɪv/ Something that motivates or encourages
n. 激勵，誘因
"Financial incentives for having children"
譯：生育的財務激勵
population n /ˌpɒp.jʊˈleɪ.ʃən/ All the people living in a country or area
n. 人口，居民
"China's population is ageing rapidly"
譯：中國人口迅速老化
policy n /ˈpɒl.ə.si/ A course of action adopted by a government
n. 政策，方針
"The one-child policy ended in 2016"
譯：獨生子女政策於 2016 年結束
