🌍 Article Context | 文章背景

This biographical article examines how Ali Khamenei came from a modest background to become Iran's supreme leader for 37 years, maintaining power through repression and strategic maneuvering at great human cost.

中文：這篇傳記文章檢視了阿里·哈梅內伊如何從卑微背景成為伊朗最高領袖並執政 37 年，通過鎮壓和戰略操縱維持權力，付出巨大的人類代價。

modest adj /ˈmɒd.ɪst/ Relatively small in size or amount; humble
adj. 謙遜的，適中的
"Khamenei came from a modest background"
譯：哈梅內伊來自卑微背景
repression n /rɪˈpreʃ.ən/ The use of force to control and subdue
n. 鎮壓，壓制
"Power maintained through repression"
譯：通過鎮壓維持權力
maneuver n/v /məˈnuː.vər/ A movement or action designed to achieve a goal
n. 策略，機動
v. 機動，操縱
"Strategic maneuvering to consolidate power"
譯：鞏固權力的戰略操縱
consolidate v /kənˈsɒl.ɪ.deɪt/ To make something stronger or more secure
v. 鞏固，加強
"Consolidating power over 37 years"
譯：37 年來鞏固權力

n. 反對，反對派
"Crushing political opposition"
譯：鎮壓政治反對派
legacy n /ˈleɡ.ə.si/ Something left or handed down from the past
n. 遺產，_legacy_
"A controversial legacy of repression"
譯：充滿爭議的鎮壓遺產
scholarship n /ˈskɒl.ə.ʃɪp/ Academic study or achievement; financial aid
n. 學問，獎學金
"Educated in Qom, a centre of Shia scholarship"
譯：在什葉派學術中心庫姆接受教育

adj. 威權的，專制的

n. 革命，變革
