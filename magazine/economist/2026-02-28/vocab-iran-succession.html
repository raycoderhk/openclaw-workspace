🌍 Article Context | 文章背景

This article analyzes the crisis in Iran following the assassination of Supreme Leader Ayatollah Ali Khamenei, examining the succession process and the myths surrounding the regime's stability.

中文：這篇文章分析了最高領袖阿亞圖拉阿里·哈梅內伊遇刺後伊朗的危機，檢視了繼承過程和圍繞政權穩定性的神話。

succession n /səkˈseʃ.ən/ The process of inheriting a position or title
n. 繼承，繼位
"The succession of Iran's supreme leader"
譯：伊朗最高領袖的繼承
perilous adj /ˈper.əl.əs/ Full of danger; risky
adj. 危險的，艱險的
"A perilous test of the regime"
譯：政權的危險考驗
assassination n /əˌsæs.ɪˈneɪ.ʃən/ The murder of a political leader
n. 暗殺，刺殺
"The assassination of the supreme leader"
譯：最高領袖的暗殺
regime n /reɪˈʒiːm/ A government, especially an authoritarian one
n. 政權，政體
"The Iranian regime faces challenges"
譯：伊朗政權面臨挑戰
stability n /stəˈbɪl.ə.ti/ The state of being stable; not likely to change
n. 穩定性，穩固
"Questions about regime stability"
譯：對政權穩定性的質疑
theocracy n /θiˈɒk.rə.si/ A system of government by religious leaders
n. 神權政治，神權國家
"Iran is an Islamic theocracy"
譯：伊朗是伊斯蘭神權國家
cleric n /ˈkler.ɪk/ A religious leader, especially in Islam or Christianity
n. 神職人員，教士
"Senior clerics choose the successor"
譯：高級神職人員選擇繼任者
successor n /səkˈses.ər/ A person who takes over a position
n. 繼任者，繼承人
"Finding a suitable successor"
譯：尋找合適的繼任者
turmoil n /tɜːˈmɔɪl/ A state of great disturbance or confusion
n. 動亂，騷亂
"Political turmoil follows the death"
譯：死亡後的政治動亂
authority n /ɔːˈθɒr.ə.ti/ The power to give orders and enforce obedience
n. 權威，權力
"The supreme leader holds ultimate authority"
譯：最高領袖擁有最終權威
