summit n /ˈsʌm.ɪt/ A meeting between heads of government
n. 峰會，最高級會議
"A summit was held to discuss trade"
譯：舉行峰會討論貿易
coalition n /kəʊ.əˈlɪʃ.ən/ A temporary alliance of political parties
n. 聯盟，聯合政府
"A coalition government was formed"
譯：聯合政府成立了
referendum n /ˌref.əˈren.dəm/ A general vote on a single political question
n. 全民公投
"A referendum was called on the issue"
譯：就此問題舉行了全民公投
incumbent adj/n /ɪnˈkʌm.bənt/ Currently holding office
adj. 現任的 n. 現任者
"The incumbent president sought re-election"
譯：現任總統尋求連任
sanctions n /ˈsæŋk.ʃənz/ Penalties imposed to pressure a country
n. 制裁，處罰
"New sanctions were announced"
譯：宣佈了新制裁
diplomacy n /dɪˈpləʊ.mə.si/ The practice of conducting international relations
n. 外交，外交手腕
"Diplomacy efforts continued"
譯：外交努力繼續進行
coup n /kuː/ A sudden illegal seizure of power
n. 政變
"A military coup overthrew the government"
譯：軍事政變推翻了政府
treaty n /ˈtriː.ti/ A formal agreement between states
n. 條約，協定
"A peace treaty was signed"
譯：和平條約被簽署
embassy n /ˈem.bə.si/ The official residence of an ambassador
n. 大使館
"The embassy was evacuated"
譯：大使館被撤離
constituency n /kənˈstɪtʃ.u.ən.si/ A body of voters in a specified area
n. 選區，選民
"He visited his constituency"
譯：他訪問了他的選區
