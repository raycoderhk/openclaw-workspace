🌍 Article Context | 文章背景

The Weekly Cartoon is a political satire cartoon that humorously comments on current events and political situations around the world, using visual metaphors and wit.

中文：每週漫畫是一幅政治諷刺漫畫，使用視覺隱喻和機智幽默地評論全球時事和政治情況。

satire n /ˈsæt.aɪər/ The use of humor to criticize people's stupidity
n. 諷刺，諷刺作品
"Political satire is popular"
譯：政治諷刺很受歡迎
cartoon n /kɑːˈtuːn/ A simple drawing telling a story or making a point
n. 漫畫，卡通
"The weekly cartoon comments on politics"
譯：每週漫畫評論政治
metaphor n /ˈmet.ə.fɔː/ A figure of speech comparing two unlike things
n. 隱喻，暗喻
"Visual metaphors are used"
譯：使用視覺隱喻
caricature n /ˈkær.ɪ.kə.tʃʊər/ A picture that exaggerates features for comic effect
n. 諷刺畫，漫畫
"A caricature of the politician"
譯：政治家的諷刺畫
commentary n /ˈkɒm.ən.tər.i/ A descriptive account of an event
n. 評論，註釋
"Social commentary through art"
譯：通過藝術進行社會評論
irony n /ˈaɪ.rə.ni/ The expression of meaning using opposite words
n. 諷刺，反語
"The irony was not lost on readers"
譯：讀者都明白其中的諷刺
depict v /dɪˈpɪkt/ To represent by a drawing or painting
v. 描繪，描述
"The cartoon depicts world leaders"
譯：漫畫描繪了世界領導人
exaggerate v /ɪɡˈzædʒ.ə.reɪt/ To represent something as larger than it is
v. 誇張，誇大
"Features are exaggerated for effect"
譯：為效果誇大特徵
wit n /wɪt/ The capacity for inventive thought; clever humor
n. 機智，風趣
"The cartoon shows great wit"
譯：漫畫展現了極大的機智
current adj /ˈkʌr.ənt/ Belonging to the present time
adj. 當前的，現行的
"Current events are depicted"
譯：描繪了當前事件
