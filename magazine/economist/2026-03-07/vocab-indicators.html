This section provides comprehensive economic and financial data including GDP growth rates, inflation figures, unemployment rates, stock market indices, and commodity prices from countries around the world.

你的句子
indicator n /ˈɪn.dɪ.keɪ.tər/ A thing that indicates the state or level of something
n. 指標，指示器
"Economic indicators showed improvement"
譯：經濟指標顯示改善
GDP n /ˌdʒiː.diːˈpiː/ Gross Domestic Product; total value of goods produced
n. 國內生產總值
"GDP growth was 2.5% last year"
譯：去年 GDP 增長為 2.5%
inflation n /ɪnˈfleɪ.ʃən/ A general increase in prices
n. 通貨膨脹
"Inflation rose to 3%"
譯：通脹上升至 3%
unemployment n /ˌʌn.ɪmˈplɔɪ.mənt/ The state of not having a job
n. 失業
"Unemployment fell to 4%"
譯：失業率降至 4%
surplus n /ˈsɜː.pləs/ An amount left over; excess
n. 盈餘，順差
"Trade surplus increased"
譯：貿易順差增加
deficit n /ˈdef.ɪ.sɪt/ The amount by which something is too small
n. 赤字，逆差
"Budget deficit widened"
譯：預算赤字擴大
currency n /ˈkʌr.ən.si/ A system of money in general use
n. 貨幣，通貨
"The currency weakened"
譯：貨幣貶值
commodity n /kəˈmɒd.ə.ti/ A raw material that can be bought and sold
n. 商品，貨物
"Commodity prices rose"
譯：商品價格上漲
index n /ˈɪn.deks/ A statistical measure of change
n. 指數，指標
"Stock market index climbed"
譯：股票市場指數上漲
quarterly adj /ˈkwɔː.təl.i/ Happening four times a year
adj. 季度的，每季度的
"Quarterly results were released"
譯：季度業績公佈
