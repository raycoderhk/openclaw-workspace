📰 Article:China sets its lowest growth target for a generation
📂 Section:China

🌍 Article Context | 文章背景

This article reports on China's decision to set its lowest economic growth target in decades, reflecting challenges in the economy including property sector struggles and weak consumer demand.

中文：這篇文章報導了中國決定設定幾十年來最低的經濟增長目標，反映了經濟面臨的挑戰，包括房地產行業困境和消費需求疲軟。
