🌍 Article Context

This Leaders article argues that China should set a higher GDP growth target to avoid a fourth year of deflation. The article analyzes China's economic challenges, including weak domestic demand, property sector struggles, and the need for more aggressive fiscal stimulus to maintain economic momentum.

📝 Quick Quiz

1. What does "deflation" mean?

✅ Correct! Deflation means a general decline in prices.
❌ Not quite. Deflation is the opposite of inflation.

2. What is "fiscal" policy?

✅ Correct! Fiscal policy involves government spending and taxes.
❌ Not quite. Fiscal policy is about government budgets.

3. What does "ambitious" describe?

✅ Correct! Ambitious means requiring great effort.
❌ Not quite. Ambitious means challenging.
