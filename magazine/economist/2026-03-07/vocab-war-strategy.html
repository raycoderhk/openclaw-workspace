🌍 Article Context | 文章背景

This cover story analyzes the ongoing Middle East conflict, arguing that military actions lack a coherent long-term strategy, potentially leading to prolonged instability and unintended consequences.

中文：這篇封面故事分析了持續的中東衝突，認為軍事行動缺乏連貫的長期戰略，可能導致長期不穩定和意外後果。
