🌍 Article Context | 文章背景

This article argues that understanding corporate behavior and firm-level dynamics is essential to explaining why some countries experience economic growth while others stagnate.

中文：這篇文章認為，理解企業行為和公司層面的動態對於解釋為何一些國家經歷經濟增長而另一些國家停滯不前至關重要。
